SEO & Launch Checklist

Page Titles & Meta:

Use a unique title tag and meta description for every page.
Home: Ashikur Rahaman | Web3 BD Leader, Founder & Blockchain Product Builder
About: About | Ashikur Rahaman – Web3 & Fintech Entrepreneur
Projects: Projects | Ashikur Rahaman – Blockchain & AI Products
Services: Services | Ashikur Rahaman – Web3 Growth & Product Services
Contact: Contact | Ashikur Rahaman – Let’s Build the Future of Web3

Meta descriptions should stay concise and keyword-rich.
Example: “Portfolio of Ashikur Rahaman, Web3 business development executive and founder of SpudBlocks & Saga Lab, focused on blockchain, fintech, AI SaaS, and strategic partnerships.”

Open Graph / Twitter Cards:

Use matching og:title and twitter:title for each page.
Use matching og:description and twitter:description.
Add og:image and twitter:image with a 1200x630 sharing image.
Set og:url to the canonical page URL.
Use og:type="website" and twitter:card="summary_large_image".

robots.txt & Sitemap:

Allow crawling in robots.txt.
Generate a sitemap.xml covering all public pages.
Submit the sitemap to Google Search Console.

Canonicalization:

Add a canonical link on each page to the main URL.

Analytics & Verification:

Add GA4 or a privacy-friendly analytics script.
Add Google Site Verification if needed.
Track Resume, Contact, and Case Study clicks.

Structured Data Testing:

Validate JSON-LD with Google Rich Results Test or Schema Markup Validator.
Fix any schema errors before launch.

Accessibility Testing:

Check contrast, alt text, keyboard navigation, and ARIA with Lighthouse or axe.
Ensure every interactive element is keyboard reachable.

Performance Testing:

Run Lighthouse on mobile and desktop.
Aim for strong LCP, low CLS, and minimal JS.
Optimize the hero image with Next Image and set explicit dimensions.

Cross-Browser:

Test in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
Check mobile layouts and interactions.

Final Content Review:

Proofread all copy.
Confirm links, resume download, and contact forms work.

Launch:

Deploy to the production domain and verify the live site.
