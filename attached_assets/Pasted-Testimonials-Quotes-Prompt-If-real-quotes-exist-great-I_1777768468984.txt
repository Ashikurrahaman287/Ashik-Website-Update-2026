3. Design & UI Brief

Overall Theme: Dark, professional palette with neon/tech accent colors for a modern Web3 and AI feel.
Color Palette:
Primary BG: #0E131F or #121827
Accent 1: #4C84FF
Accent 2: #00FFC7 or #FF4C4C
Text: #FFFFFF for headings, #A0A0A0 for body text
Buttons: Accent color backgrounds with white text and subtle hover glow.
Typography:
Headings: Bold geometric sans-serif
Body: Clean sans-serif
Layout & Spacing:
Hero: Full-screen above the fold with text on one side and a portrait or illustration on the other.
Grid: Responsive 12-column layout.
Cards: Glassy, rounded-corner cards with hover lift.
Sections: Clear separation and generous spacing.
Responsive Behavior:
Navigation collapses into hamburger menu on mobile.
Timeline becomes a vertical list on small screens.
Project cards stack in one column on narrow screens.
Ensure touch targets are large enough for accessibility.
Interactions & Animations:
Use subtle fade-in scroll animations, animated counters for stats, hover glow on cards, and lightweight loading states.
